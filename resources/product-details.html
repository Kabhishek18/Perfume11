Jimmy Choo Perfume by Jimmy Choo, Step out into the world every morning feeling bold, confident and alluring when you're wrapped in the luminous Jimmy Choo. This mesmerizing women's fragrance combines fruity, green, floral and gourmand accords for a fresh yet decadent concoction that's bound to make a powerful first impression. A blend of juicy pear, mandarin orange and a variety of green notes creates a lush, radiant ambience that immediately sets a mood, while a sultry heart of tiger orchid infuses an even greater air of exotic sensuality. Closing the enchanting fragrance are base notes of creamy toffee and Indonesian patchouli, layering in a deep, heady foundation that's sure to gain the attention of anyone nearby.
